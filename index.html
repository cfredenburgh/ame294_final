About

Chelsea Fredenburgh is a student at Arizona State University pursuing a degree in Digital Culture.

She has been an active employee for Apple since 2011 creating positive customer experiences and staying up to date with the latest technology. In her freetime she is venturing in fine arts, animation, and game development.

Wii Airband

This group project consisted of creating an experiential system that utilized Max/MSP
and the use of a tool. The tool we decided to go with was a Wiimote controller. With the help of Max/MSP, we executed a system that successfully transformed the Wiimote into four different instruments.

  • Class: Digital Culture
  • Date: December 2014
